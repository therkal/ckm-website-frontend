Corrina & Kenneth Martens

The Meeting

Corrina and Kenneth met in 2020 through their biggest common passion: photography. They met during the Covid-19 pandemic -- Kenneth lived in the Netherands, while Corrina was situated in the US. During that time, they communicated daily being incredibly complex given time zones. In the summer of 2021 things started looking up for the international couple, and some of the travel restrictions were lifted. They were finally able to be physically together. They overcame the distance, and decided to be together permanently. They married in February and currently reside in the United States with their three cats: Rou, Stevie, and Willow.

Besides photography, Corrina and Kenneth are passionate about wildlife, ecology, zoology, and travel. On one of their most recent trips to North and South Dakota, they met Steve and Kathy Leonardi who inspired them to take their passion for photography and travel to the next level and combine them in a business. With Steve's help, the couple started their newest and biggest adventure yet!

Corrina

Corrina double majored in Zoology and Environmental Science at Miami University, where she graduated with her Bachelor’s Degree in 2013. As an undergraduate, she did research on the genetic diversity of the European wall lizard and interactions with adenylate cyclase toxins. After graduation, she attended the University of Cincinnati to pursue her Master of Science in Biological Sciences, with a focus in Reproductive Biology. She was awarded the University Graduate Scholarship for doing research in collaboration with the Cincinnati Zoo & Botanic Garden and Cincinnati Children’s Hospital on polar bear reproduction.
